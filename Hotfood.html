Cooking and baking.

Basic waffle batter recipe.

  1. 1 and 3/4 cups of all purpose flour.
  2. 2 tablespoons of sugar
  3. 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  4. 2 eggs
  5. 1 and 3/4 cups of milk
  6. 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions for making a simple waffle.

  1. Combine your dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat wet ingredients and then add the dry , stirring JUST until moistened.
  3. Pour evenly into your waffle iron.
  4. Cook until golden brown or is how ever you like it.

Strawberry cream cheese muffins

  1. 8oz of cream cheese, softened
  2. 1/2 stick of butter
  3. 1 1/2 cups of sugar
  4. 2 eggs
  5. 1/2 cup of milk
  6. 1 teaspoon of vanilla flavoring/extract
  7. 2 cups of all purpose flour
  8. 1 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
  9. 1/4 teaspoons of salt
  10. 1 cup of chopped strawberries.(fresh)

Baking instructions.

  1. Preheat oven 350 to degrees
  2. Cream together cream cheese, butter and sugar in a top-mixer. Add eggs,milk and vanilla and mix until just combined.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together flour,baking powder,baking soda and salt.Add dry ingredients to mixing bowl and mix until just combined.Do not over mix.Fold in strawberries.
  4. Spoon batter into paper lined muffin pans.Fill each cup about 3.4 high.Bake for about 20-25 minutes, until tops are golden.Cool on rack for at least 15 minutes.

Basic strawberry coulis

  1. 1 cup of frozen unsweetened strawberries
  2. 1/2 cup sugar
  3. 1 teaspoon lemon juice

How to make the coulis

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the strawberries and sugar and lemon juice. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Transfer to a blender.
  2. Puree until smooth, then strain in a strainer, and set aside.

Fun fact, this will be able to last you in cold storage for a week.
